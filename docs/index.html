Anatomy of an OKR
OBJECTIVE
Create clear career pathways across Product Engineering.
KEY RESULTS
KR1
Publish a career framework covering all Product Engineering roles.
KR2
75% of colleagues have a documented development plan by end of quarter.
KR3
90% of colleagues report they understand their progression opportunities.
INITIATIVES
Career framework design
Mentoring programme
Manager coaching
Career workshops
The most common mistake
Do not measure effort. Measure change.
ACTIVITIES — not OKRs
· Launch a mentoring programme
· Run career workshops
· Improve onboarding documentation
· Hold quarterly reviews
OUTCOMES — these are OKRs
✓ 80% of colleagues have an active mentor
✓ 90% understand their career options
✓ Median onboarding falls from 8 to 5 weeks
✓ 85% report feeling supported in their growth
